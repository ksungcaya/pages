Transportation preparations (reserve/buy + non‑IC)

  • IC + connectivity: Set up Mobile Suica (or buy ICOCA) at KIX; load ¥5,000–10,000. 7‑Eleven ATMs work with foreign cards. Pick up eSIM/Wi‑Fi at KIX.
  • Seat reservations (point‑to‑point is most cost‑effective; JR Pass not worth it here):
    • 11/25 Shinkansen Kyoto→Nagoya (SmartEX/app or machines).
    • 11/25 Ltd. Exp Shinano Nagoya→Nakatsugawa (or JR Rapid if no reservation).
    • 11/26 Ltd. Exp Shinano Nagiso→Nagoya.
    • 11/26 Shinkansen Nagoya→Tokyo.
    • 11/27 JR Chuo Ltd. Exp (Kaiji/Azusa) Otsuki→Shinjuku (return from Fuji).
    • 12/2 Keisei Skyliner Keisei Ueno→Narita T2 (reserve day before).
    • Optional: Fuji Excursion train or Shinjuku↔Kawaguchiko highway bus (reserve).
  • Cash/not IC: Nakatsugawa→Magome bus (coins), Magome→Tsumago same‑day bag transfer (cash), Katsuo‑ji admission (~¥500), small temple fees/stalls.
  • Nearest stations: Osaka hotel ↔ Osaka/Umeda 6–10 min walk; Kyoto ↔ Gion‑Shijo (Keihan) 2–4 min; Tokyo ↔ Ueno/Okachimachi 6–10 min; ↔ Keisei Ueno ~12–15 min.

Fuji decision & logistics (compact)

Pick the best day

  • Favor: day after rain, high pressure, N–NW winds, low humidity, overnight lows ~0–5°C at Kawaguchiko.
  • Avoid: warm humid S winds, showers, “mostly cloudy” AM, low cloud ceiling over Yamanashi.
  • Late Nov pattern: clearest dawn–10:00; clouds build late morning; sometimes clears near sunset.

Quick checks (bookmark)

  • Fujigoko live cams (multi-angles, watch 5–10 min)
  • Yamanashi Mt. Fuji Live View
  • Tenki.jp Fujikawaguchiko hourly (cloud 0–30% at 6:00–12:00 = good)
  • JMA town forecast (Fujikawaguchiko)
  • Windy.com (Clouds low/mid; Wind surface/1500 m)

Go / no-go timeline

  • T–24h (night before, 20:00–22:00): if 6:00–12:00 cloud ≤30–40% with N winds → go; if ≥70% and southerlies → plan to swap (primary swap: Sun 11/30; secondary: Fri 11/28).
  • Morning-of (05:30–06:15): live cams clear on ≥2 cameras for 5–10 min → depart; fully socked-in to noon → postpone.
  • En route (Otsuki 08:00–08:30): recheck cams; if turned to >70% cloud to midday → pivot back to Tokyo backups.

On-the-ground order (maximize clear window)

  • 1) Chureito (08:30–10:00) — if clear, you’ve got your Fuji.
  • 2) Kachi‑Kachi Ropeway (09:00–11:00) — wind may suspend; swap to Oishi Park.
  • 3) Oishi/Nagisa Park — midday panoramas/reflections; later: Momiji Corridor/Tunnel; sunset silhouette 16:30–17:00 if you stay.

Read the sky fast

  • Cap (lenticular) cloud on summit: windy aloft but Fuji visible — still go.
  • Low gray deck hiding cone: likely poor till front passes — postpone.
  • Haze only: climb a bit (ropeway/tenjōyama trail) or use a CPL filter.

Ropeway and wind

  • Kachi‑Kachi often halts if lake‑level gusts >12–15 m/s (check Windy). Keep Oishi/Nagisa as backup.

Transport flexibility

  • Prefer trains on foliage weekends: JR Chuo → Otsuki → Fujikyu (or all‑reserved Fuji Excursion).
  • Return: reserve JR Limited Express (Kaiji/Azusa) Otsuki→Shinjuku for a guaranteed seat.

Kawaguchiko buses (Red Line essentials)

  • Buy 1‑day pass at Kawaguchiko Station (cost‑effective for 3–4+ rides; IC ok if not).
  • Key stops: 1 Station; 9 Ropeway; 22 Oishi Park; 19 Itchiku Kubota; 18 Museum of Art (for Momiji Corridor).
  • Flow tips: buses bunch midday; add ±10 min per ride; walk lakeside between 18/19/ Corridor if crowded; taxi Station↔Oishi ~12–15 min, ¥2,000–¥2,500.

If you must move the Fuji day

  • Best swaps: Sun 11/30 (your Yanaka day is easy to slide) or Fri 11/28 (swap with Yokohama/Kamakura if that day looks crystal).
  • Crowds heavy both weekend days — trains beat buses.

Bookings (when needed)

  • Local/rapid JR to Otsuki + Fujikyu locals: no reservation; IC ok.
  • Fuji Excursion (direct Shinjuku↔Kawaguchiko): reservation required.
  • JR Limited Express return (Kaiji/Azusa Otsuki→Shinjuku): recommended to reserve.
  • Book via JR EAST Train Reservation (web/app; 1 month prior, 10:00 JST) or at JR machines/counters.
  • Highway bus alt (Busta Shinjuku↔Kawaguchiko): highway-buses.jp (reserve; traffic‑prone).

Micro‑tips

  • Arrive early — first 2 hours are gold. Dress warm (gloves).
  • Don’t chase clouds midday; enjoy Itchiku Kubota or cafés and keep watching cams for late clearing.

Fast checklist

  • Bookmark: Fujigoko cams; Yamanashi cam; Tenki.jp hourly; Windy (cloud/wind).
  • Night before: pick lowest AM cloud + N winds.
  • 05:30–06:15 cams → go/no‑go. On site: Chureito → Ropeway (if running) → Oishi/Nagisa → optional sunset.
  • Reserve return Limited Express if staying past lunch.
